# BD Research Kit — Claude Desktop Version

## Setup (30 seconds)

1. Create a folder on your computer for BD research work, e.g. `~/Documents/bd-research/`
2. Copy the `CLAUDE.md` file from this folder into that directory
3. In Claude Desktop, open that folder as a project (File > Open Folder, or drag it in)

That's it. Claude now knows about Further, our partnership categories, and how to structure research output.

## Usage

Just ask naturally. Claude will automatically pick the right research workflow:

| What you want | What to say |
|---------------|-------------|
| Company deep-dive | "Research Zillow as a potential partner" |
| Evaluate a deal | "Evaluate a partnership with Blend for mortgage API integration" |
| Map a market | "Map the competitive landscape for title insurance" |
| Broad scan | "Scan the mortgage technology startup space for BD opportunities" |
| Write a memo | "Write a partnership memo for Blend — white-label mortgage calculator" |

## Tips

- **Be specific.** "Research Blend Labs mortgage API" works better than "Research Blend"
- **Chain your research.** Start broad ("scan proptech startups"), then narrow ("research Blend"), then formalize ("write a partnership memo for Blend")
- **Ask follow-ups.** After any research output, ask Claude to dig deeper on specific aspects
- **Request documents.** Ask Claude to save research as a file, or format it for copy-paste into Google Docs

## What Claude Knows

The CLAUDE.md file gives Claude permanent context about:
- What Further does, our stage, funding, revenue model
- Our partner categories ranked by priority
- Research methodology and source quality standards
- Five structured output formats (company profile, partnership evaluation, competitive landscape, market scan, partnership memo)
- Confidence labeling (HIGH/MEDIUM/LOW)
- Security guidelines (no secrets, confidential by default)

## Updating

If the CLAUDE.md gets updated, just replace the file in your project folder. No reinstall needed.
